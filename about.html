Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American actor, internet personality, and computer programmer. He is best known for making comedic YouTube videos, which include his satirical acting and skits. Some of his known works include Merman Academy, NicheZeke, Lucid Nonsense, Theories with Zeke, and The Witches.
