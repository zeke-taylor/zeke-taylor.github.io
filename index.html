Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American actor, internet personality, and musician. He is best known for his roles as Jaden Newman in the American fantasy comedy web-television series Merman Academy. And he has also gained notability through his web-television documentary series Theories with Zeke, his role as Hakeem Zeki in the American television series Lucid Nonsense.
