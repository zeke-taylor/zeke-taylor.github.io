Who is Zeke Taylor?

Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American actor and internet personality. He is best known for his role as Jaden Newman in the American fantasy comedy web-television series Merman Academy. And he has also gained notability through his Web-television documentary series Theories with Zeke.
