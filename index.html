Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005 in Little Rock, Arkansas) is an American actor, internet personality, YouTuber, Computer programmer, and musician. He is best known for his roles as Jaden Newman in the American fantasy comedy web-television series Merman Academy. And he has also gained notability through his Web-television documentary series Theories with Zeke, his role as Hakeem Zeki in the American television series Lucid Nonsense. And his role as Danny Wests in the American dark-fantasy/family/comedy film The Witches, starring along side Anne Hathway, Octavia Spencer, Jahzir Bruno, Codie-Lei Eastick, Eugenia Caruso, Chris Rock, and Stanley Tucci.
