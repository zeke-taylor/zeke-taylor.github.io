Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American actor, internet personality, and musician. He is best known for his role as the main character Jaden Newman in the American fantasy comedy web television series Merman Academy, as well as his roles in NicheZeke, Theories with Zeke, and Hakeem Zeki in Lucid Nonsense.

Career

Zeke Taylor started acting in 2015 after he created a web television series called Merman Academy. He starred in it as the main character and protagonist, as well as directed it, produced it, and edited it. The show (Merman Academy) lasted for three seasons and 27 episodes. Ever since, Taylor's career has seemed to be doing great for him. The young actor has even hinted at new-upcoming projects. This year, it was announced that Zeke Taylor would make his film debut in summer of 2020 on June 24, 2020 when he released a new film called Merman Academy: The Movie where he starred as the main character Jaden Newman. Taylor will also be playing the role of Reynard in the upcoming 2021 horror film Cybernard.
