Zeke Taylor (born December 29, 2005) is an American actor and internet personality He is best known for his role as Hakeem Zeki in the dark supernatural drama web-television series Lucid Nonsense, as well as his roles of Easton Zeki in the fantasy short film Outsider: The Movie and it's spin off series The Chaos, his role as Jaden Newman in the web television fantasy series Merman Academy.

Career

Zeke Taylor started acting in 2015 after he created a web television series called Merman Academy. He starred in it as the main character and protagonist, as well as directed it, produced it, and edited it. The show lasted for three seasons and 27 episodes. Taylor's career seems to be doing great for him. The young actor has even hinted at upcoming projects. Zeke Taylor is now best known for his role as the leading character, Hakeem Zeki, in the dark fantasy supernatural drama web-television series Lucid Nonsense. In 2021, Taylor starred in the fantasy short film Outsider: The Movie as Easton Zeki alongside co-stars Zyon Christie and Joshua Day-Stevens, who portrayed Michael Davis, the latter of whom also created, wrote, directed, and edited the film. He reprised his role of Easton Zeki in a crossover event between Lucid Nonsense and Outsider: The Movie, the two birthed a spin-off series called The Chaos, which went into development in early 2022. Taylor reprises both roles in the spin-off series as Easton, a main character, and Hakeem as a recurring character, it premiered in 2025.
