Ezekiel Jermaine Shephard-Taylor (born December 29, 2005), popularly known as Zeke Taylor is an American actor and internet personality. He is best known for his role as the main character Jaden Newman in the American fantasy comedy web-television series Merman Academy, as well as his roles in NicheZeke, Theories with Zeke, Lucid Nonsense and The Witches. Zeke Taylor started acting in 2015 after he created a web-television series called Merman Academy. He starred in it as the main character and protagonist, as well as directed it, produced it, and edited it. Merman Academy lasted for three seasons and 27 episodes. Ever since, Taylor's career has seemed to be doing great for him. The young actor has even hinted at new-upcoming projects. This year, it was announced that Zeke Taylor would make his film debut as Danny Wests in The Witches, the remake of The Witches (1990) which is based off the book of the same name by Roald Dahl that was released in 1983. The Witches (2020) reboot is also known to be starring Anne Hathaway, Octavia Spencer, Codie-Lei Eastick, Jahzir Bruno, and Chris Rock. The film is set to be released on October 9th, 2020.
