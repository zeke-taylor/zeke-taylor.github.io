Biography

Zeke Taylor is an American Actor and Internet personality. He is best known for NicheZeke (2013), Merman Academy (2015), Lucid Nonsense (2019), Theories With Zeke (2019), and The Witches (2020). Taylor was born as Ezekiel Jermaine Shephard-Taylor on December 29, 2005 in Little Rock, Arkansas. His interest in acting began at the age of eight, after a lot of television shows and movies with his mom. Not long after, Taylor created a youtube channel and began uploading short videos and skits of him singing, dancing, and doing many other things.

Career

Zeke Taylor has starred in several television shows and films.
Beginning of acting career:
Zeke began his acting career back in 2015 after he created a web-television series called Merman Academy. He starred in it as the main character and protagonist, as well as directed it, produced it, and edited it. The show ran for three seasons and 27 episodes over the course of airing. Ever since, Taylor's career has seemed to be doing great for him. The young actor has even hinted at new-upcoming projects.
Film Debut:
This year, it was announced that Zeke Taylor would make his film career debut as Danny Wests in The Witches, the remake of The Witches (1990) which is based off the book of the same name by Roald Dahl that was released in 1983. The Witches (2020) reboot is also known to be starring Anne Hathaway, Octavia Spencer, Codie-Lei Eastick, Jahzir Bruno, and Chris Rock. The film is set to be released on OCtober 9th, 2020.
